Arcane

When friends ask me what types of shows I want to work on, Arcane is the show that I'll always enthuse about first. There's so much to love: its painterly style, how it combines 2D painted backrounds with 3D character models, and its wondeful soundtrack. As a result, it's inspired a lot of great fanart.

I adore Leyendecker’s art, and this piece beautifully captures his style: mid-century typography and imagery combined with hard edge brushwork

Breathtaking brushwork! I’d love to learn how to paint like this, filling the canvas with vibrant colors and chaotic textures

Transforming what would be a regular illustration into something more with a sprinkle of graphic design tickles my imagination
